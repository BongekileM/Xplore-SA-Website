Durban

Durban, formerly known as Port Natal, is the largest city of South Africa’s KwaZulu-Natal province. The city of Durban’s sunny weather and warm Indian Ocean make it the ultimate holiday destination for travellers. Durban is also home to Ushaka Marine World, the fifth-largest aquarium in the world. Ushaka Marine World boasts an aquarium, water park, and a village walk for shopping. This city has it all, the seas, the valleys, the mountains; and with the city’s warm beaches, visitors can enjoy snorkelling.
