Johannesburg

Johannesburg, or Joburg or Jozi as the locals refer to it, is not only the biggest city in South Africa, but also the capital of South Africa’s wealthiest province, Gauteng. South Africa’s Golden city is home to one of the coolest neighbourhoods around the world called “Maboneng’’. The city also boasts of its phenomenal museums that include the Apartheid Museum.
